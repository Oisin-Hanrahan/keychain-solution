Solution Overview

Keychain – AI for CPG Product Creation & Supplier Sourcing

Keychain is an AI-powered platform for consumer packaged goods (CPG) brands and retailers to rapidly create new products and match with qualified suppliers. Users describe what they want in natural language, and Keychain generates a structured product specification and identifies relevant manufacturers, packaging partners, ingredient suppliers, and logistics providers.

The platform centralizes supplier discovery, communication, documentation, and task tracking in a single interface, reducing time-to-market and manual sourcing overhead.

Built on Google Cloud

Google Cloud Architecture

Keychain is built entirely on Google Cloud to ensure scalability, reliability, and enterprise-grade security.

Core Services

  • Vertex AI – LLM-driven specification generation, semantic supplier search, recommendation models.
  • Cloud Run – Stateless backend APIs serving web and service traffic.
  • Cloud Functions – Event-driven and asynchronous processing for workflows.

Data & Storage

  • BigQuery / Firestore – Supplier records, product specifications, and historical activity.
  • Cloud Storage – Document and asset storage for R&D and supplier collaboration.

Security & Operations

  • IAM / VPC / KMS – Access control, network isolation, and encryption.
  • Cloud Logging / Monitoring – Observability, auditing, and reliability.

Business Impact

Business Value

By automating product specification generation and supplier matching, Keychain reduces the time and effort required to launch new CPG products. The platform:

  • Shortens product development cycles from weeks to days.
  • Improves accuracy of supplier selection and compliance checks.
  • Eliminates fragmented communication across email, spreadsheets, and offline tools.
  • Provides a structured, data-driven view of supply options and constraints.
